To drop all flows that come in from port s1-eth2:
sudo ovs-ofctl add-flow s1 "in_port=2, actions=drop"

To forward all flows that come in from port s1-eth1 to s1-eth3:
sudo ovs-ofctl add-flow s1 "in_port=1, actions=output:3"
